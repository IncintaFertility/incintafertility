How quickly can I be seen?

Most new patients can be scheduled within 1-2 weeks. For urgent cases or specific concerns, we often accommodate same-day appointments when possible.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes! We offer secure telehealth consultations for initial meetings, follow-up appointments, and second opinions. This is especially convenient for patients who live far from our locations.

What makes Incinta Fertility different?

Our combination of cutting-edge technology, personalized care, multiple convenient locations, and exceptional success rates sets us apart. We focus on treating you as a whole person, not just a diagnosis.

How much does fertility treatment cost?

Costs vary based on your specific treatment plan. We provide transparent pricing and work with most insurance plans. Our financial counselors will review all options during your consultation.
